ABOUT THE ADARSHA HOSTEL

and what makes it Great!

CREATING THE

BEST SPACE

TO FIND THE PURPOSE

At The Student Hotel, we are committed to creating state-of-the-art facilities colourful experiences we believe will change the way we live, work and travel and that redefine our understanding of what makes a ‘student’.

HOME FOR SUCCESS


It is a common perception that, hostel life has a unique impact on the pattern of students’ life. Living in the hostel makes students socially and behaviorally different. Boarding or hostel life is a combination of different cultural backgrounds, in hostel life, students learn to live with different cultural background people. There is a popular quotation “Times change people change” it is best applicable for the hostel students. Hostel's life going to change the way a student is, its effect on personality behavior, thinking, and dressing as well.

We understand that university years aren't just plain sailing. But we do believe that by having the right surroundings and support, you will be able to navigate successfully through the ups as well as the downs of this exciting, yet sometimes challenging journey. This is why we call Adarsha hostel is a "Home for Success".

OUR MISSION

We aim to create the best space where students can find their purpose and change the world. Not just with the well-designed and thought-out rooms, or the many great shared communal facilities. When we think “ best space ,” we think of a stimulating environment and an exciting global community What we discovered along the way, is this type of atmosphere inspires all who encounter it. The Student Hostel is a place where anyone with a student spirit – curious, open, free, adventurous – can thrive.

COMPLETE CONNECTED COMMUNITY

So we reimagined our hostels as boundary-blurring spaces where our students could connect in smart design co-living and co-working spaces. We redefine the living experience of students by building an ecosystem that offers more than just accommodation, our Complete Connected Community.
